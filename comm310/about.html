All About Me...

Who is JaggerNaught? Well you wont have to try very hard to find my real name, I'm not very careful with it but the tl;dr is that I'm a wannabe comedian who is trying to leave his mark on as many people as possible. I've always been pretty good at making people smile, and this is my life's quest. It started out with being the class clown in grade school, then I found YouTube and naturally made my way to Twitch. Eventually, I want to find my way to the live stage and become a stand up comedian. Until then, and until I strike it rich in the online community, I am a Web Developer and Designer with my wife, working for a small web development company. Gotta pay them bills! I have a 2 year old son and am living the dream. Thats enough about me, if you wanna know more, drop my a stream or leave a comment on a video, I'd be happy to chat!
